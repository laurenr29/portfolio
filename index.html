Lauren's Content Design Guidelines

A collection of core UX writing rules, facts, and key metrics that shape Lauren's content work.

Plain Language Principle

Never use industry jargon where a simple word will do. Plain language is accessible to all reading comprehension levels and speeds translation.

15+ Years

Lauren has designed high-impact digital copy, guidelines, and user navigation maps since 2011.

Progressive Disclosure

Show users all the information they need, but not all at once. Break complex financial or medical inputs across clean layout sequences.

Global Alignment

Led content strategy across multi-layered teams at Verizon and U.S. Bank, standardizing microcopy frameworks across design systems.

Accessibility (A11y) first

Write for screen readers first. Ensure buttons have clear accessibility labels, maps have text lists, and forms have descriptive errors.
